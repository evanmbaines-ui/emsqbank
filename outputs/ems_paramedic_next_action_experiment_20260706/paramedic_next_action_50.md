Purpose: experimental NRP-style clinical judgment set focused on short stems, paramedic-level next actions, order of operations, and protocol/rule application.

Selection note: topics were selected from exact unwritten clinical production job rows. Most came from clinical content IDs not present in prior completed or website packets; the remainder came from exact unused clinical concept rows in previously touched sections to reach 50 total.

This packet intentionally does not apply the normal EMSqbank doctrine or audit workflow.

Revision note: distractors were revised to favor plausible correct-later actions, partial bundles, or adjacent protocol steps that get priority or sequence wrong.
